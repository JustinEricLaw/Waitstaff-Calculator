Calculate your tips with ease!
  1. Head to the “New Meal” section to enter a price, tax, and tip amount for each meal.
  2. Use the submit button to add each meal to your earnings. Use the clear button to clear the fields and add additional meals.
  3. Switch to the “Earnings” section to view your total earnings for all meals combined.
  4. Click the reset button to clear all earnings and start over.
